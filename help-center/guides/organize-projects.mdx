## Overview

Structure your docs for quick and easy access.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    ````
    Click "New Project" and give it a clear name.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add folders">
    ````
    Use folders like "API Docs", "Guides", "FAQs" to group pages.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    ````
    Go to project settings > Users > Invite by email.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
